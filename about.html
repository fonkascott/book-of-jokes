Mental School

Two mentally disturbed men Scott and Kwame decided they must go to school.
They collected old books and went to sit under the tree pretending it was a school.
The following day, Scott got there early and climbed the tree.
As Kwame came and saw his friend on top of the tree he asked, "What are you doing up there?"
Scott replied, "I am now in high school."
